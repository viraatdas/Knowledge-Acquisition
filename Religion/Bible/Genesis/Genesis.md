# Genesis Overview

## Summary
Genesis is the first book of the Bible, detailing the creation of the world, the early history of humanity, and the origins of the Israelites.

## Major Themes
- Creation
- Fall of Man
- Flood
- Patriarchs (Abraham, Isaac, Jacob)
- Joseph's story
